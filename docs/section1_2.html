Results of Five and Six Model Ensemble Comparison

Below are the results from a comprehensive test of ensemble configurations and voting strategies. The test covered hard voting, soft voting, calibrated soft voting, weighted soft voting (equal, recall-based, stable, and custom weighting schemes), and threshold-aware approaches (probability-threshold sweeps plus priority-threshold logistic stacking variants). The test was done for a five model ensemble (QuadraticDiscriminantAnalysis, AdaBoostClassifier, DecisionTreeClassifier, RandomForestClassifier, and ExtraTreesClassifier) and a six model ensemble (QuadraticDiscriminantAnalysis, AdaBoostClassifier, DecisionTreeClassifier, RandomForestClassifier, ExtraTreesClassifier, and XGBRFClassifier). Highlighted rows indicate they are included in the scatter plot and have accuracies greater than 0.70. The link for notebook containing the test can be found here.
