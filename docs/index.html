The Project

Originally conceived as a project to visualize WWII convoy equations, this project has grown into a data-driven quantitative study of factors that made Allied convoys vulnerable during the Battle of the Atlantic. By applying machine learning to archival data, the project predicts whether each convoy was at high or low risk of ship losses. Current analyses cover the HX, SC, OB, ON, and ONS convoy series, with plans to expand further. Supporting work includes feature analysis, visualization, and a history section, all aimed at understanding the dynamics of WWII's longest battle.
